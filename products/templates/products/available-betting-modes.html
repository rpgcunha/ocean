Available betting modes

Currently we have 4 available betting modes on our platform, they are:

Coinflip

Coinflip is a betting mode that involves flipping a virtual coin. Two players bet on the outcome of the coin flip (heads or tails) and the winner takes the entire pot.

Lottery

The game is played by choosing six numbers from a pool of 60 numbers. Players can choose their own numbers or have them randomly selected by the system. Draws will initially be held once a week, on Sunday, and the numbers are drawn using our fully Fair and scam-free system, containing its Hash. To win the jackpot, players must match all six numbers. If there are no winners for a particular draw, the jackpot rolls over to the next draw. In addition to the jackpot, there are also prizes for matching five, four, three, two and one numbers. The prize money is distributed among the winners of each category.

Crash

This popular betting mode consists of placing a bet and withdrawing it before the boat explodes, and the multiplier, as well as the risk, increases over time.

Double

The Double betting mode is a probability-based betting game in which players bet on one of 3 colors: green, red, and black. Green pays 14X the bet amount, red and black pay 2X. The player has the option to "double" their bet multiple times, thus increasing the potential for winnings but also increasing the risk of loss. The objective is to choose the correct color.
